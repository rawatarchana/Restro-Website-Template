Restro is a trendy restaurant that opened its doors in 2021, situated in the bustling neighborhood of Malvir Nagar in Goa. The ingredients used in each dish are of the highest quality, ensuring a flavorful and satisfying meal.Overall, Restro is a must-visit destination for food enthusiasts who are looking for a memorable dining experience in Goa.
